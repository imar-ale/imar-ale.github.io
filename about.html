A little bit
about us

Come and taste the fantastic flavours of authentic gelato in our charming courtyard! Our delicious gelato and sorbet is made fresh everyday for us. The shop is a cutesy little parlour, tucked away in a brick lane in Parnell.

You can enjoy the sunshine outside or enjoy the indoor seating area.

There are more than 20 flavours of gelato and sorbet to give you plenty of choice. There is also a range of hot and cold drinks available in the shop.

We open late for great coffee and delicious desserts such as; brownies and sundaes. Or indulge in our delicious and freshly made hot waffles with the gelato of your choice.
